    About Us

    Design isn't just about what looks good.
    It's about what works.

    I am Divine Owoyemi, a Visual Identity Designer and Brand Strategist focused on helping businesses build brands that are clear, memorable, and built to grow.

    For more than eight years, I have worked with startups, founders, and growing companies to create visual identities that do more than just look good. Every brand I design is built around strategy first. The goal is always the same: help a business stand out, connect with the right audience, and compete confidently in its market.

    My experience spans brand identity design, visual systems, logo development, brand positioning, and creative direction. Over the years, I have worked on projects across different industries including fintech, digital products, small businesses, and growing personal brands. I have also won two national hackathon awards for my design work, which reflects not just creativity, but the ability to think strategically and solve real problems through design.

    What makes my work different is the focus on clarity and structure. I do not just design logos. I help businesses understand how their brand should look, feel, communicate, and position itself in the market. Every project is approached with the mindset of building a long-term brand, not just creating visuals.

    Today, my focus is working with serious business owners and founders who want a strong, professional brand identity that reflects the quality of what they offer. Whether it is a startup building from scratch or an existing business ready to reposition, the aim is always to create a brand that feels intentional, premium, and built for long-term growth.
